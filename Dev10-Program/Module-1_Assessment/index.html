About Me

I can confidently say that I've above average life and career experience. I've lived a few countries and learned new languages. After college, I worked for a financial institution where I leaned about investment and retirement savings. As a recent college graduate at the time, I also realized that accounts with millions are not only in movies. For the last eight years I worked in public service witnessing the flip side of life. There are people who live in chronic poverty even in the most developed place in the world.

In the last year and half I came to realize IT is a very valuable skill to have. So between full time job and other life commitments like watching funny cat videos, I invested a respectable amount of time, money, and energy preparing myself for a new , hopefully ,and mostly likely final career. I am looking forward to hands on and real world experience to become a skilled software developer.
